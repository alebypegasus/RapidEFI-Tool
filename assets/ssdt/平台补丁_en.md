## Platform Patch (SSDT Platform)

RapidSSDT automatically determines the required SSDTs based on your platform specifications (CPU Intel/AMD, Desktop, Laptop, Mini PC, Server).

Ref: [Dortania ACPI Guide](https://dortania.github.io/Getting-Started-With-ACPI/ssdt-platform.html#desktop)

---

### RapidSSDT Legend:
- **Basic (Official Recommend)**: Core required SSDTs.
- **Recommend (Feature Fixes)**: Recommended feature patches.
- **Optional (Enhancements)**: Optional system enhancements.
- **Select All**: Toggles all SSDTs (core, recommended, optional).
- **Custom SSDT**: Generates tailored SSDTs compiled directly against your extracted DSDT/SSDT tables.
- **Preset SSDT**: Generates universal preset SSDTs following official OpenCore guidelines.
